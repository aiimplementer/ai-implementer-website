5 Simple AI Automations Every Small Business Can Implement Today

AI can handle 30–40% of daily tasks. Here are five easy automations you can set up today.

1. Auto-reply to customer queries using ChatGPT or Gemini

Set up an AI assistant to handle common questions. It saves staff time and improves response speed.

2. Automate invoices with Google Sheets + Apps Script

Generate and send invoices automatically when new entries appear in your sheet.

3. Schedule meetings using AI assistants

Tools like Motion or Reclaim.ai schedule and reschedule automatically based on availability.

4. AI-powered FAQ bot for your website

Build a simple FAQ bot trained from your knowledge base. It handles repetitive queries 24/7.

5. Smart lead sorting using AI + CRM integration

Use AI to score and prioritize leads automatically inside your CRM.
