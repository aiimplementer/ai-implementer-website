How to Automate Customer Support Without Hiring a Big Team

Learn how to automate customer support with AI chatbots and FAQs to deliver faster responses 24x7.

Manual responses and inconsistent answers frustrate customers. Automation can deliver fast, consistent support without expanding your team.

Problem

Small teams struggle with manual responses, delays, and inconsistent answers that harm customer experience.

Solution

Use an AI chatbot and FAQ system integrated with your website or WhatsApp. Train it using your support data for accurate replies.

Tools

  • ChatGPT API
  • Make.com / Zapier
  • Google Sheets (training data)

Benefits

  • Faster response time
  • 24x7 availability
  • Reduced support cost
