5 Simple AI Automations Every Small Business Can Implement Today

AI can handle a large portion of daily repetitive tasks. Here are five practical automations you can implement quickly.

1. Auto-reply to customer queries using ChatGPT or Gemini

Set up an AI assistant to handle common customer questions on your website or WhatsApp. This reduces response time and frees up staff from repetitive replies.

2. Automate invoices with Google Sheets + Apps Script

Use a spreadsheet as the single source of truth: generate invoices automatically when rows are updated and email them to clients with minimal manual effort.

3. Schedule meetings using AI assistants

Tools like Motion or Reclaim.ai can coordinate schedules automatically and suggest optimal meeting slots, removing the tedious back-and-forth.

4. AI-powered FAQ bot for your website

Build a small FAQ bot trained from your knowledge base or Google Sheets. It answers repeat questions and routes complex queries to humans.

5. Smart lead sorting using AI + CRM integration

Use AI to score leads based on responses and behavior, then push top leads automatically into your CRM for priority follow-up.

Start small, measure results, and expand the automations that deliver the highest ROI.
