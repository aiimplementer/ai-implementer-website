Automated LinkedIn / Facebook Posting Agent

Category: Marketing & Social Automation AI

The Automated LinkedIn / Facebook Posting solution helps businesses and professionals stay active on social media without manual effort. Posts can be scheduled or triggered directly from a WhatsApp interface, offering unmatched convenience and personalization.

Using simple WhatsApp commands, users can draft, preview, and post updates instantly — or schedule them for later. The system integrates with LinkedIn and Facebook APIs and supports auto-caption generation and content tagging to maximize engagement.

Key Features

  • WhatsApp-triggered post creation
  • LinkedIn & Facebook integration
  • Scheduling and auto-posting options
  • Smart caption generation
  • Content personalization by topic or target

How It Works

  1. User sends a message via WhatsApp
  2. AI generates or refines post content
  3. User confirms or edits the draft
  4. AI schedules or posts instantly to LinkedIn/Facebook (if required email approval step can be added)
  5. Confirmation and engagement summary shared back via WhatsApp

Benefits

  • Zero manual posting effort
  • Maintain consistent online presence
  • Boost social engagement automatically
  • Convenient WhatsApp-based control
  • Time-efficient content management
